Cebu, where the heart sings

Welcome To Cebu

Cebu is a long and narrow island that stretches 196 km from north to south, and 32 km from east to west at its widest point. Located on the eastern central area of the island, Cebu City is its main urban centre.

Economy of Cebu

Cebu is the dominant and most prosperous. Fifty-six percent of Metro Cebu's total economic output is due to industry. Services account for 36 percent. As a mountainous island, agriculture accounts for only eight percent of its output.

Celebrate Cebu

Last January 15, 2020, during The Gala held at the Grand Convention Center, Cebu City announced its new Tourism brand “Celebrate Cebu.”

Cebu and its Tourism

Known for its historical sites, it also offers eco-adventures such as waterfalls and coral reefs. Locals tend to run several businesses during the week, and enjoy boating and scuba diving on weekends.

Positioning Strategy

How does Cebu became attrative?

Cebu is the Philippines' oldest city and the center of trade, business, and tourism in the Visayas. Cebu offers a wealth of activities for both business and pleasure, with old Spanish forts, amazing marine parks, delectable cuisine, and bustling business parks.

Cebu is a true "power island" in every respect. Take a look at its SRP (South Road Properties), a 300-hectare reclaimed land area, as proof of its expansion. In addition, the island is a leader in the Philippines' sustainable tourism movement. The Mactan-Cebu International Airport is the country's first solar-powered airport, and its concessionaires are prohibited from using single-use plastics. From award-winning mangrove river experiences to tours of ancestral houses led by local guides, visitors can enjoy mindful tourism. Prepare your taste buds for a pleasant surprise as Cebu's culinary legacy is equally amazing

This metropolitan destination provides eco-adventures in addition to historical monuments, luxurious resorts, and a dynamic nightlife. Waterfalls and unusual birds await anyone brave enough to go into the inner woods of Cebu. Coral reefs almost encircle the whole perimeter of this 200-kilometer-long island. It's not unusual to see residents, who are self-employed, running many enterprises throughout the week and going boating and dive diving on weekends.

During pandemic, travellers are expected to have roundtrip plane ticket, filled-out Cebu public health passenger locator form, medical certificate indicating that the traveler does not exhibit symptoms of the COVID-19 disease at the point of origin. The medical certificate should be issued within 24 hours before arrival. Also. fullly vaccinated* individuals are exempted from presenting a medical certificate. They must instead present a vaccination card or certificate that shows their fully vaccinated status, proof of pre-booking at accredited Cebu Province hotels or resorts and passed symptom.
